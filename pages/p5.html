Data standardization

Data standardization is the process of rescaling one or more attributes so that they have a mean value of 0
and a standard deviation of 1.

Standardization assumes that your data has a Gaussian (bell curve) distribution. This does not strictly have
to be true, but the technique is more effective if your attribute distribution is Gaussian.
